About White Church

The White Church, officially known as St. Anne's Church, is a historic church located in the cantonment area of Indore. Built in the late 19th century, this colonial-era church is renowned for its pristine white facade and neo-gothic architecture. The church's interior features stained glass windows, wooden pews, and ornate altars, creating a serene and tranquil ambiance for worshippers. St. Anne's Church serves as a place of worship for the Christian community in Indore and also attracts visitors with its architectural beauty and historical significance.
