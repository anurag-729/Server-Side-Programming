About Kanch Mandir

Kanch Mandir, also known as the Temple of Glass, is a unique architectural marvel located in Indore. Built in the early 20th century by Seth Hukumchand Jain, this Jain temple is renowned for its exquisite glasswork. The interiors of Kanch Mandir are adorned with intricate glass mosaics, paintings, and sculptures, creating a mesmerizing visual spectacle. The temple's sanctum sanctorum houses the idols of Lord Mahavira and other Jain deities, attracting devotees and tourists alike. Kanch Mandir is not only a place of worship but also a testament to the artistic brilliance and craftsmanship of its creators.
