About Central Museum

The Central Museum, also known as the Indore Museum, is a treasure trove of art, history, and culture. Established in the late 19th century, the museum houses a diverse collection of artifacts, ranging from ancient sculptures and coins to paintings and manuscripts. Exhibits at the museum showcase the rich cultural heritage of Madhya Pradesh, including the contributions of various dynasties that ruled the region. The museum's galleries offer visitors a fascinating journey through time, providing insights into the art, architecture, and traditions of Indore and its surrounding regions.
