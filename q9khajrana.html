About Khajrana Ganesh Mandir

Khajrana Ganesh Mandir is one of the most revered temples in Indore, dedicated to Lord Ganesh. Situated in the Khajrana area, the temple is believed to have been established in the early 20th century. What makes this temple unique is its architecture, adorned with intricate carvings and sculptures. Devotees from all over the country visit this temple to seek the blessings of Lord Ganesh, especially during festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi. The atmosphere around the temple is filled with spirituality and devotion, making it a significant cultural and religious landmark in Indore.
