About Lal Bagh Palace

Lal Bagh Palace is an architectural masterpiece that showcases the grandeur and magnificence of the Holkar dynasty. Built in the 19th century, this sprawling palace is a fine example of European-style architecture, adorned with intricate carvings, marble columns, and Italian marble floors. The palace complex encompasses vast gardens, fountains, and a museum that houses an extensive collection of artifacts, including paintings, sculptures, and royal memorabilia. Lal Bagh Palace provides a glimpse into the luxurious lifestyle of the Holkar rulers and serves as a testament to their cultural patronage and artistic endeavors.
