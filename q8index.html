Welcome to IET DAVV, Indore!



Institute of Engineering & Technology Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, (IET-DAVV) Indore started its academic journey with glorious dreams on 04 September 1996. It is an approved Institution by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, Government of Madhya Pradesh and University Grants Commission (UGC). IET is academically autonomous with flexible and modern curriculum in six engineering branches. , with Master of Engineering Programmes with six specializations. and one MSc program in Applied Mathematics.

IET-DAVV as a research centre has established itself in a very short duration with research scholars from reputed organizations such as SGSITS, AICTE, RRCAT, NRCS, IIITA, etc. have registered and obtained PhD's. Numerous research publications at national and international level by the faculty in a year. Honour of being Editors, Referee and member of editorial board of reputed journals. MoUs signed with NIC, RRCAT, TCS and Eicher Motors for knowledge exchange.
